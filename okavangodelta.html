Okavango Delta

The Okavango is very good for birders as huge numbers of waterfowl and raptors reside there, but you also find elephant, zebra, buffalo, wildebeest, giraffe, hippo, crocodile, lion and kudu.

Best time to visit

July to October (Best wildlife viewing)

High season

July to October (Higher rates apply but not busy)

Best weather

May to August (Less hot during the day)
